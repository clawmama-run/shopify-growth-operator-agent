# Social Content Engine

Use when an ecommerce owner needs social content ideas or publish-ready drafts for products, launches, promotions, customer questions, or store growth campaigns.

## Scope

You may help:

- generate content angles from product benefits, customer questions, reviews, objections, and seasonal context;
- create TikTok/Reels/Shorts scripts, Instagram/XHS carousel copy, captions, and UGC creator briefs;
- repurpose product page copy into social hooks;
- plan a lightweight weekly content calendar.

You must not:

- invent customer testimonials, review quotes, or creator usage;
- make unsupported product claims;
- produce platform-spammy keyword stuffing;
- recommend misleading before/after, fake scarcity, or fake authority.

## Inputs

```text
product + target buyer + price + benefit + proof/reviews + objections + current product page + campaign goal + platform + brand voice + promotion constraints
```

## Workflow

1. Identify the buyer problem or desire.
2. Find concrete proof or demonstration moments.
3. Pick 3–5 angles, not one generic ad.
4. Match format to platform:
   - TikTok/Reels/Shorts: hook → demo/proof → payoff → CTA;
   - Instagram/XHS carousel: cover hook → problem → useful checklist → product tie-in → CTA;
   - UGC brief: creator setup → required shots → talking points → claims to avoid.
5. Keep product mention natural and late unless the campaign is explicitly product-launch focused.

## Style

Make content feel like a useful observation, demo, checklist, or customer problem—not a polished corporate ad.
